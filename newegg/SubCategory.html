Looking for a powerful, ready-to-use computer for the home or office? A desktop PC here refers to a completely pre-built desktop computer, which is the antithesis of the self-built or DIY (do it yourself) desktop computer system. Desktop PCs are offered by brand name manufacturers, like ABS, Hewlett-Packard, Compaq, Acer, Lenovo, Gateway and Sony VAIO or SIs (Systems Integrators) and are typically offered with full system warranties.

Pre-built computers represent great choices for users that require convenience, performance and ease-of-use. Computer hardware knowledge is not a necessity. Brand name desktop PCs are capable of delivering all the functions and performance you need for your applications, including web browsing, enjoying picture and movie files and office work, as well as multimedia processing and professional 3D work. In addition, desktop PCs still allow for extensive upgrades to be performed, allowing you to make the most out of your investment.
